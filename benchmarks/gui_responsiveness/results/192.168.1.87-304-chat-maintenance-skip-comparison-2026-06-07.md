## What changed

- The benchmark now reports `callbackMs`, `postCallbackFrameWaitMs`, `pollWorkMs`, `pollOverheadMs`, `pollFetchMs`, `pollJsonMs`, and `pollClientMs` so headless frame-settle waits are separated from actual poll work.
- The benchmark now includes `poll-cached:*` samples. These keep the current ETag and directly exercise conditional `304 Not Modified` polls.
- Chat workspace `304` polls no longer call `renderChat(...allowPollSkip)`. They run only lightweight unchanged-state maintenance: topbar context, change summary, auto-dismiss, and drawer sync.
